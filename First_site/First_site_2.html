Archer Street

Is the place to go for a Saturday night out in Soho. It has two rooms both with great music. Upstairs is the place to be through with singers making sure you keep the energy going.

When to go: Special ccassion

The Prince of Wales

Plenty of options in this place with two floors of music and a dreamy roof top to cool down and catch up.

When to go: Post saturday day drinkng in Pop Brixton

Adventure Bar

One of Claphams many places to be seen, a small dance area and a long bar makes this place perfect for atmosphere and getting served. There is also a small space out front for those drunk chats.

When to go: Night out with the girls

The Old Queen's Head

downstairs is the quintessentual pub but upstairs is where this place makes it's mark. With a massive dance floor this place is great to finish a night with the music going untill 3am.

When to go: Group night out

Blues Kitchen

A perfect place to start your Saturday night. With great live music and delcious food where you can get in the mood for a great night.

When to go: Looking for fun night but with a little sophistication
